Purpose

1: Decide what hairpin structure to use when specifying an aptamer that specifically binds to the spike protein(1:スパイクたんぱく質と特異的に結合するようなアプタマーを指定した際に、どのようなヘアピン構造を使用するかを決定)

2:When determining the hairpin structure, find out if it actually causes the HCR reaction(ヘアピン構造を決定した際に、それが実際にHCR反応を起こすかを調べる)

Determining the hairpin structure(ヘアピンの決定)

For 1.We　use "NUPACK" to determin the hairpin structure.Simulation with NUPACK For the following strand combination, simulation carried out using NUPACK. H1, H2, Initiator (H1, H2, Initiator = 1.0 μM) The length of the stem and loop was adjusted with reference.(1) The uploaded figure is below.

Next, carry out the design.

The result of structural stability between 4-90˚C are shown below

We found that this structure is stable at room temperature. This result does not affect the test kit at home.

This is stability of H1-H2-Initiator structure at 24˚C. We confirmed that the structure did not break. Check if the reaction proceeds with Visual DSD.

Looking at the time change of such a reaction, it looks like this(このような反応の時間変化を見るとこのようになる)

It is true that the concentration of the hairpin structure decreases as the concentration of the inisiator decreases, indicating that HCR occurs without any problem. 確かにinisiatorの濃度減少とともにヘアピン構造の濃度が減っており、問題なくHCRが起きていることがわかる。

However, in reality, hairpins do not only cause HCR, but also cause leaks reaction that unlock and react by themselves. Therefore, next, consider the reaction considering the leak.
